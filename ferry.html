Ferry Flights

Our Ferry Services

We offer ferry flight services and ferry pilots for hire across Europe, Africa and the Middle East. All of our ferry pilots have several years of professional flying experience in piston aircraft (single and multi) over long distances, complimented by extensive biz-jet, charter and airline flying experience. Our team’s skill mix also includes several years flight operations experience prior to flying professionally.


We are currently able to offer our services in the following categories of aircraft:

  • Microlights
  • Motor Gliders(TMG)
  • Single Engine Pison(SEP)
  • Multi Engine Piston(MEP)
  • Single Engine Turbine(SET)
  • King Air
  • BAe Jetstream 41
  • DHC Dash8 100/200/300/400

Our breadth of knowledge is our strength. Every ferry pilot in our network has a minimum of 1500hrs flying experience. Most of our pilots have remained active as instructors and glider towing pilots, as well as operating ferry flights and commercial airline pilots. We will always operate your aircraft to the highest safety standards; our team members are dedicated and passionate about the industry. We know our stuff.

Our price structure is honest and upfront. You can use our ferry cost calculator or get in touch for a bespoke quote. We hate nasty surprises, so always agree a final price before the trip commences.


Safety is a key priority. You should never book a ferry flight without reviewing the credentials of your ferry pilot, and we provide these every time. Our flights are fully insured, and for peace of mind you will be able to track your aircraft online for the duration of its journey. Our ferry pilots aren’t a one-man band, our flight operations team oversee journeys to ensure any issues are quickly rectified. We’ve got your back.
