Ferry Flights

Our Ferry Services

We are able to offer ferry flights across Europe, Africa, Middle East through to Asia & Australia. Additionally, we can source and enable fitting of ferry tanks for transatlantic flights. All of our pilots have years of experience flying piston aircraft (single and multi) over long distances, complimented by extensive bizjet, charter and airline flying experience. Two of our team members also have several years flight operations experience prior to flying professionally.


We are currently able to offer our services in the following categories of aircraft:

  • Microlights
  • Motor Gliders(TMG)
  • Single Engine Pison(SEP)
  • Multi Engine Piston(MEP)
  • Single Engine Turbine(SET)
  • BAe Jetstream 41
  • DHC Dash8 100/200/300/400

Our pilots all have solid and wide-ranging flying backgrounds, all have a minimum of 2000hrs flying experience. Most of our pilots have remained active as instructors, glider towing pilots alongside ferry flying – alongside operating as professional pilots. We will always operate your aircraft to the highest safety standards; our team members are out of the box thinkers who will get the job done on time and to budget.


We will agree a final price with you before the trip commences you can rest assured you will not be hit with unexpected costs upon delivery. Our flights are fully insured, you will be shown the credentials of your ferry pilot before the trip commences. You will be able to track your aircraft online for the duration of its journey. Our pilots have the full backup of our operations department to ensure issues can be rectified quickly and plan’s B & C are already ready and waiting.
