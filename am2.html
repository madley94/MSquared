About us

MSquared is a small, closely knit team of experienced professionals. Aiming to bring quality, professional and transparent ferry and flight operations services to the light aircraft market. Based in Edinburgh, Scotland but with our team spread across western Europe – we each have experience ferrying various types of aircraft internationally. Allowing us to work with you to develop a cost-effective ferry plan.


Why should you use us?

By offering fair, transparent and straightforward pricing along with clarity on the whole ferry process. Providing you with our pilot’s credentials before accepting a quote, then giving you access to our tracking portal. With a daily brief available as the journey progresses. Providing our pilots with the best equipment as appropriate to the job and the operations backup to allow them to focus on flying.


We operate on a low cost, high value basis. You may be able to find a cheaper quote, using less experienced pilots or having pilots operate as a one-man band. Our ops team having worked professionally in both flight operations and as professional pilots. This allows us to give full support to our ferry crews, without the requirement to maintain full time staff or associated office costs.


Why did we set up M² Aviation?

Born out of hardship, our network of ferry pilots has been brought together as a result of the coronavirus pandemic and ensuing economic downturn. We’re a hardworking team with experience from across the aviation sector. We love what we do, which is why M² Aviation has been established. Our values are based upon our experience in the sector. We promise to deliver a safe, cost efficient, and commercially minded aircraft ferry service.
