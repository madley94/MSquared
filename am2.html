M² Aviation

Individual. Dedicated. Professional.

Born out of hardship, our network of ferry pilots has been brought together as a result of the coronavirus pandemic and ensuing economic downturn. We’re a hardworking team with experience from across the aviation sector, which has been so badly affected by this crisis. Our skies may be a little quieter, but our skills will not go to waste. We love what we do, which is why M² Aviation has been established to keep our dedicated team in the skies. Our values are based upon our experience in the sector. We promise to deliver a safe, cost efficient, and commercially minded aircraft ferry service.
