Meet the Team

Mike Osborne


Mike has been flying for over 10 years with 6 years’ experience working within the Aviation industry including light aircraft & business jet flight operations and has flown the Jetstream 41 and Dash 8 commercially for several years alongside flying as a glider tow pilot. He has experience flying light aircraft across Europe and Africa, additionally to this he enjoys flying microlights and motor gliders. Mike is a well-rounded and experienced pilot in both commercial and general aviation, he takes pride in completing his operations in a safe and efficient manner. In his spare time, he enjoys playing the ukulele, taking his motorbike around the UK and travelling around Europe with his girlfriend..

Luke Pickup


Luke joins us with over 6 years’ flying experience, he has a wide range of flying experience flying the Let410, Jetstream 41, ERJ and Boeing 747 commercially. He is currently operating as a Jetstream 41 Captain and has extensive ferry flight experience in these categories of aircraft. In his spare time, Luke enjoys cooking, mountain biking and tending to his cars.

Ash Basnett


Ash joins us with over 12 years flying experience, bringing with him a wealth of single engine & multi engine piston and single engine turbine hours. He has several thousand hours in single engine piston aircraft across Europe, has operated the PC12 into some of the most challenging airports in Europe (looking at you Courchevel!) and has taught several of the UK RAF’s Red Arrows display pilots. Ash maintains a keen enthusiasm for flying and flight safety, he is a multi-engine instrument instructor and examiner. In his spare time Ash enjoys adding to his whisky collection and playing his electric guitar..
