Meet the Team

Our experience and knowledge is important to us, which is why your aircraft will never be in the hands of a low-hours pilot. Collectively, our roster of ferry pilots has flown over 40 types of aircraft over 30,000 hours.

We currently have a small, experienced team available for hire and flight operations support. Every pilot in our network has a minimum of 1500hrs flight experience and has operated in a professional capacity. Based in the UK and Western Europe, our team has experience flying light aircraft for commercial surveying; glider towing; flight instructing; ferry flights and of course, flying for fun. Our pilots have extensive experience of independent planning, coordinating and operation of ferry flights. We’re skilled, passionate, and know our industry inside and out.


Mike Osborne


A well-rounded and experienced pilot in both commercial and general aviation, Mike O. is passionate about all things up in the air. In his spare time you will catch him flying microlights and motor gliders, or as a tug pilot for his local gliding club. With over 10 years of flying experience, Mike has flown the Jetstream 41 and Dash-8 commercially for one of Europe’s largest regional airlines and has several years’ experience in corporate flight operations. He has flown light aircraft across Europe and Africa. He’s a safe pair of hands who truly loves his job.

Mike Hadley


With a over a decade of flying experience, and six years within the aviation industry, Mike has worked his way up. From instruction his local gliding squadron to flight ops and aircraft dispatch. Prior to spending 3 years flying the Boeing 737 for Europe’s largest airline, Mike coordinated the ferry operations of a large flight school in Europe and the Middle East. He built his hours flying SEP and MEP aircraft around Europe, the US and Canada, enjoying aerobatics and briefly venturing into rotary flying. When faced with a new challenge, he brought together M² to utilise his repertoire of skills, experience and passion for aviation.

Ash Basnett


Our resident dare devil, Ash has experience of operating the Pilatus PC12 in some of the most challenging airports in Europe (looking at you Courchevel!) and has taught several of the RAF’s Red Arrows display team. He has over 12 years flying experience in single & multi engine piston, and single engine turbine. Ash has boundless enthusiasm for flying and is an expert in flight safety, he is a multi-engine instrument instructor and examiner.

Luke Pickup


Like an encyclopaedia of knowledge, Luke has a wide range of flying experience, including the Let410, Jetstream 41, ERJ, and Boeing 747. He has over six years commercial flying experience, currently operating as a Jetstream 41 captain. He’s a ferry flight champion, with extensive experience in these categories of aircraft.
